ASUS OLED CARE

SCREEN PROTECTION

ASUS OLED Care provides users with timely pixel cleaning reminders, a screen saver, a screen move feature, and more to protect the OLED panel and ensure the longevity of the screen.

Screen Saver

Automatically dims the screen after a two minutes of inactivity.

Pixel Cleaning

Recalibrates the screen when the monitor has been active for a period of several hours. The process takes just a few minutes, and activates automatically when the monitor is turned off*.

Screen Move

Pixel locations are slightly shifted periodically to help prevent burn-in. There are several movement levels to choose from.

Logo Brightness Adjustment

The monitor automatically detects static onscreen logos and reduces their brightness to correct for potential picture-quality issues.

*Do not unplug the power cord during this time, or else pixel cleaning will be interrupted.

UNIFORM BRIGHTNESS

When activated, the ROG-exclusive Uniform Brightness setting within the OSD menu decreases peak brightness to keep levels consistent for better viewing, even when changing the size of bright white windows. It also makes marathon gaming sessions much more comfortable on the eyes.
